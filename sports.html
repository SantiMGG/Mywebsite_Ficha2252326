Downhill is one of the most risky sports in the world and one of the modalities of mountain biking that consists of descending as quickly as possible on a natural track, which has obstacles, which may well be natural or also artificial. The winner is the one who makes the shortest possible time on the timed descent.

The beginnings

Sport began in the United States in the late 70s, more precisely in the state of California. The motorcyclists did the same downhill test as it is known today, but the use of this vehicle was prohibited due to the damage it caused, which led them to change their motorcycles for bicycles in order to continue experiencing the adrenaline that they so much enjoyed. liked. At first, bicycles were not suitable for practicing this sport, since in better conditions you need bicycles with front and rear suspensions with shock absorbers and hydraulic oil, as well as disc brakes, wider tires and chainring or chain guide protectors.

Safety first

Downhill bikes are very different from conventional bikes because they are much heavier, so there is more speed on the descent. Athletes must carry all protective equipment to avoid injury after a fall. The main recommendation to be an outstanding athlete in dowhnill is that the speed during the descent is progressive. It is not convenient to go out at the highest speed, but the ideal is to go step by step to obtain the expected results.
