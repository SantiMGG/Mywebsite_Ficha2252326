Sport is a physical activity that is exercised as a game or competition, the practice of which involves training and subject to previously established rules. Within the world of sport there are many different disciplines. Some are specific to certain parts of the world, others are more common worldwide; However, to learn more about them, you have to start with the basics ... There are several types of sports, usually these can be divided into four major categories according to the different types of skills they require: physical sports, such as swimming or gymnastics; motorized sports, which usually involve motorized vehicles, such as motorcycle racing; mind sports, which require critical thinking skills such as chess or checkers; and sports with animals, such as horse riding.
